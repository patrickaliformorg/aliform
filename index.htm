We're a web company specialising in Rich Internet Applications.

Aliform Ltd is a web technology company based in central London. We design and develop cutting edge web sites and internet applications to standards endorsed by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). Our core technologies are Adobe Flex and Flash, Microsoft ASP.NET and standards-compliant HTML/CSS. Contact us for more information.

Featured Project: Natural History Museum, Darwin Centre
