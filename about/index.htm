What we do

Aliform Ltd was founded in 2004 and is based near the City in central London. We are a company of developers and designers with extensive experience in web applications, web sites and web services.

We provide a complete web development service from design and specification to build and implementation – and finally testing. Principally a business to consumer agency, we are also available to work with other agencies that require our specific skills.

Our core staff and expansive network of web professionals will ensure your project is designed, developed, tested and deployed to high standards at competitive prices.

Core staff
